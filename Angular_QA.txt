üåü Data Binding in Angular
‚úÖ Data binding connects your template (HTML) and your component (TypeScript class).
‚úÖ It keeps the view and the data in sync automatically.

üéØ Four Main Types of Data Binding
Let‚Äôs break them down:

1Ô∏è‚É£ Interpolation
Display data from the component to the view.

Syntax: {{ propertyName }}

Example:

Copy

‚úÖ The [] mean: one-way binding from component to DOM.
