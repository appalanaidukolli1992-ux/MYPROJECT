Job Description

Are you a self-starter with a passion for databases and a developer’s mindset? We’re building a brand-new team in Malmö, and we’re looking for a strong,
experienced database professional to take the lead as our first team member.

In this role, you’ll work closely with a skilled team based in India—not to take over, but to take control and drive collaboration.
 You’ll help shape the technical direction, establish best practices, and lay the foundation for a team that will grow to 4–5 people.

 

Lead database development and administration efforts, with a strong focus on Oracle (preferred), PSQL, and C#
Collaborate closely with our India-based team to align on architecture, performance, and delivery
Travel to India as part of your onboarding and ongoing collaboration
Help build a robust, cloud-ready infrastructure (Azure experience is a plus)
Contribute to a complex, long-term project with a steep learning curve—your experience and personality will be key to success

Qualifications

Minimum 5 years of experience in database development or administration
Strong expertise in Oracle and/or PSQL, with solid C# skills
A developer who truly understands databases—or a database admin with a developer’s mindset
Experience with cloud platforms (Azure preferred)
A confident, proactive personality who thrives in complex environments
Willingness to travel internationally (India)
